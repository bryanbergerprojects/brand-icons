# For Brand Owners

This repository contains SVG icons representing third-party brands. All
trademarks, logos, and brand names belong to their respective owners. Their
inclusion in this library is for purposes of identification and reference
(nominative fair use) and does not constitute endorsement, sponsorship, or
affiliation with the brands depicted.

## Requesting a takedown, modification, or correction

If you represent a brand and wish to:

- Have your icon **removed** from this library,
- Have your icon **modified** (color, shape, simplified version),
- Have your icon **corrected** (outdated logo, wrong colors),
- Restrict allowed **usage** of the icon,

please contact the maintainer through one of the channels below.

### Option 1 — Email (preferred for legal requests)

Send a message to **contact+brand-icons@bryanberger.dev** with subject:

```
[Brand Icons Removal] <Brand Name>
```

Include:

- Brand name and icon slug (e.g. `github`).
- Your role / authorization to act on behalf of the brand.
- Specific request (removal / modification / correction).
- Any relevant brand guidelines or assets to reference.

### Option 2 — GitHub issue

Open an issue using the **Trademark Removal Request** template:
[New issue](https://github.com/bryanbergerprojects/brand-icons/issues/new?template=trademark-removal.yml).

## Response timeline

We commit to acknowledging legitimate requests within **48 hours** and to
completing removals or modifications within **7 business days** of identity
verification.
