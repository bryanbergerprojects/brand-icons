## Our position

Each icon in this catalog is a redrawn or otherwise faithfully reproduced
representation of an official brand mark, used solely for the purpose of
identifying the corresponding product, service, or organization. The catalog
itself is licensed MIT for code; the artwork remains the property of its
trademark holder.

## Source of each asset

Every icon has a `meta.json` recording:

- The official source URL for each millésime
- The palette colors used in the official mark
- Notes on attribution or known variations

You can find this information on the icon's detail page (e.g. `/icon/apple`)
under the millésimes section.

## Request a change or removal

If you represent a brand and would like to:

- Correct an inaccurate rendering
- Update the catalog with a newly released mark
- Request removal of your brand from the catalog

please open an issue on
[GitHub](https://github.com/bryanbergerprojects/brand-icons/issues) or email
`contact@bryanberger.dev`. We aim to respond within 10 working days.

owners. All company, product and service names used in this catalog are for
